### Tic Tac Toe game
Two players take turns marking X or O on a grid, aiming to get three in a row vertically, horizontally, or diagonally. Here you are play it with the computer(O) and you are X. The first player to align their marks wins, while a full board with no alignments results in a draw. Optimal play from both sides always results in a tie.

### Minesweeper game
Minesweeper is a single-player puzzle game where the goal is to clear a grid of hidden squares without detonating hidden mines. Clicking reveals numbers indicating adjacent mines, or empty spaces that chain-react to reveal more board. Players use logic to deduce mine locations, placing flags on them to safely clear all non-mine cells.
